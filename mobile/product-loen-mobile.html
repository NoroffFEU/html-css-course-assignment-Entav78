KEY FEATURES

A thin and lightweight 3-layer shell jacket with mechanical stretch and taped seams.

In this jacket, the mesh lining is replaced with a tricot interior, reducing its weight and packing volume.

The jacket is made of a waterproof material with water-repellent zippers on the front and pockets. Ventilation zippers under the arms are covered by flaps.

The jacket is suitable for short and long trips in the woods, as well as for skiing.
