About Rainydays

In 2005, Rainydays was established by a group of nature enthusiasts and adventurers: Sarah and David Montgomery, alongside their close friend, Alex Chen.

Sharing a profound passion for the outdoors and experiencing firsthand the challenges of finding affordable yet durable outerwear, they set out to create a solution for like-minded individuals.

Today, Rainydays stands as a beacon in the outdoor apparel market, providing a diverse range of reasonably priced jackets that cater to the needs of outdoor enthusiasts.

Our commitment to quality, affordability, and understanding our customers` passions for hiking, exploring, skiing, camping, and canoeing has solidified our reputation as a trusted brand in the outdoor apparel industry.
